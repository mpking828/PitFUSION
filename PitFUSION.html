Data Integrity

PitFUSION fuses data from two independent sources — The Blue Alliance and Nexus FRC — each with its own update schedule, data model, and definition of "current." We reconcile these as best we can, but they don't always agree.

Queuing status, bumper colors, and next-match predictions are most likely to drift during Playoffs and Finals, where bracket assignments change in real time and Nexus may briefly stop reporting while a match is in progress.

PitFUSION is a situational awareness tool. Always confirm match assignments and bumper colors with your drive coach or the queuing staff at the field.
